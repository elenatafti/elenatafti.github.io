About Me

My name is Elena Ashtari Tafti,
I am an applied microeconomist working on topics at the intersection of innovation, health, and personnel economics.
In September 2023, I joined the Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich as an Assistant Professor in Economics.
I am a Ph.D. Scholar at the Institute of Fiscal Studies (IFS) and the Centre for Microdata Methods and Practice (CeMMAP).
