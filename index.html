About

I am an applied microeconomist working on topics at the intersection of innovation, health, and personnel economics. In September 2023, I joined the Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich as an Assistant Professor in Economics.

I am also a Ph.D. Scholar at the Institute for Fiscal Studies (IFS) and the Centre for Microdata Methods and Practice (CeMMAP).
